Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description

Fast and delicious egg for the whole family

Easy to make and budget friendly

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 4 teaspoons butter

Directions

  1. Whis eggs with salt until completely combined and very foamy.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over low heat until bubbling. Add whisked eggs; cook, stirring frequently for even, moist curds, until thickened and creamy, but still shiny, 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Removed scrambled eggs from the pan before they begin to look dry, as they will continue to thicken off the heat.
